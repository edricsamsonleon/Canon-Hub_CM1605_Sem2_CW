About the Agency

Pioneering humanity's greatest adventure beyond the stars — 90 destinations and counting.

Our Mission

The Interstellar Agency was founded with a singular vision: to make deep-space travel accessible to all of humanity. With 90 certified destinations across six cosmic categories — Nebulas, Stars, Natural Satellites, Non-Habitable Planets, Habitable Planets, and Black Holes — we offer the most comprehensive catalogue of interstellar voyages in known space.

Every destination in our catalogue has been scouted, surveyed, and certified by our elite corps of astro-navigators. From the Pillars of Creation to the event horizon of Sagittarius A*, each location is verified for safe transit.

Our Destinations

Our growing catalogue spans the known universe, organised into six categories:

  • Nebulas (15 destinations) — Stellar nurseries and supernova remnants, from the iconic Pillars of Creation to the delicate Veil Nebula.
  • Stars (15 destinations) — From our nearest neighbour Proxima Centauri to the blazing heart of Nidavellir.
  • Natural Satellites (15 destinations) — Moons, planetoids, and icy worlds, from Titan's methane lakes to the volcanic plains of Io.
  • Non-Habitable Planets (15 destinations) — Hostile, extreme, and awe-inspiring — worlds like the cube-shaped Bizarro World and the fiery pits of Apokolips.
  • Habitable Planets (15 destinations) — Earth-like worlds and exotic colonies, including Pandora, Tatooine, and the spice-fields of Arrakis.
  • Black Holes (15 destinations) — The ultimate frontier: Gargantua, M87*, and the supermassive heart of the Milky Way.

Curated Packages

We offer 15 unique travel packages, each carefully designed to showcase the full spectrum of cosmic wonders. Whether you seek the thrill of The Guardian's Odyssey, the luxury of The Cinematic Grand Tour, or the deep-space endurance of The Ultimate Completionist, every itinerary includes stops across all six categories — a true cross-section of the universe.

Our Fleet

Each destination requires a specific class of vessel. Our dispatch centre assigns the right ship from an array of specialised craft:

  • Standard Explorer / Light Courier — Short-range trips to nearby stars and nebulas
  • Endurance-Class Explorer / Deep Void Dreadnought — Long-haul cruisers for intergalactic voyages
  • Nebula-Class Cruiser / Scientific Surveyor — Specialised vessels for nebula observation
  • Event Horizon Diver / Quantum Skiff — Black hole approach craft with extreme shielding
  • Colony Ship / Spice Freighter / Light Freighter — Cargo and passenger transport for habitable worlds
  • Armored Dreadnought / UNSC Frigate / Colonial Drop Ship — Military-grade craft for high-risk zones
  • Heart of Gold / Non-Euclidean Drive Ship — Experimental vessels for exotic physics regions

Safety & Certification

Every vessel in our fleet undergoes rigorous pre-flight inspection and is certified by the Galactic Transit Authority. Our safety record is unmatched, with over 2 million successful transits and zero critical incidents. All destinations are continuously monitored for spatial anomalies.
