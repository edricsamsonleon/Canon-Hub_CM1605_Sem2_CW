About the Agency

Pioneering humanity's greatest adventure beyond the stars.

Our Mission

The Interstellar Agency was founded with a singular vision: to make deep-space travel accessible to all of humanity. We believe that the future of our species lies among the stars, and we are committed to charting safe, reliable routes across the cosmos.

From the shimmering veils of distant nebulas to the enigmatic pull of black holes, every destination in our catalogue has been scouted, surveyed, and certified by our elite corps of astro-navigators.

Our History

Established in 2145, the Interstellar Agency began as a small consortium of astrophysicists, engineers, and dreamers. What started as a research outpost on Luna has since grown into the premier interstellar travel authority, operating routes across six known sectors of the galaxy.

Today, we operate a fleet of over 200 vessels, employ 15,000 crew members, and have transported more than 2 million passengers to destinations across the known universe.

Our Fleet

Our fleet consists of four primary vessel classes, each designed for specific mission profiles:

  • Odyssey X — Long-haul cruiser for intergalactic voyages
  • Comet Flyer — Fast recon craft for rapid transit
  • Void Drifter — Stealth explorer for deep-space observation
  • Stellar Clipper — Luxury liner for premium class travel

Safety & Certification

Every vessel in our fleet undergoes rigorous pre-flight inspection and is certified by the Galactic Transit Authority. Our safety record is unmatched, with over 2 million successful transits and zero critical incidents.
